About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered non-profit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility, located in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. We rehabilitate these animals and then release them back into the wild, if possible.

Wild Rescues is a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association.
